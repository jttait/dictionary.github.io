Software Engineering Dictionary

Refactoring, noun a change made to the internal structure of software to make it easier to understand and cheaper to modify without changing its observable behaviour. Refactoring: Improving the Design of Existing Code by Martin Fowler.

Refactoring, verb to restructure software by applying a series of refactorings without changing its observable behaviour. Refactoring: Improving the Design of Existing Code by Martin Fowler.
